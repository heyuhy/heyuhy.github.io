Short Bio

I am working toward my Master degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Jianxin Li. I received my BS degree also in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2017. My research focuses on text mining and graph mining for emerging data intensive applications, with a special interest on developing HINs (Heterogeneous Information Networks) representation learning methods for large-scale real-world tasks.
