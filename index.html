Short Bio

I am working toward my Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Shuai Ma. I received my BS degree also in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2014. Sponsored by the China Scholarship Council, I was a visitting student at the Data Mining group at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, supervised by Dr. Hui Xiong from September 2017 to April 2018. Since May 2018, I have been a research intern at Baidu Business Intelligence Lab. My research focuses on graph and AI algorithms for emerging data intensive applications, with a special interest on developing network representation learning methods for mobile analytics.
